Semantic Similarity

This app calculates a measure of semantic similarity (dot product of the word embeddings) between a target word and a list of "positive" attribute word versus a set of "negative" attribute words. Based on word2vec and the gensim package. This kind of method was used in Gladwin (2022) (for further reading, see references).

Input

Please enter the words as a comma-separated list.

Target words: Positive words: Negative words:

Example

An example to run a list of emotional words through a "valence filter":
  • Target words: angry, furious, satisfied, joyous, depressed
  • Positive words: good, positive, healthy, happy
  • Negative words: bad, negative, diseased, sad
You should see the positive emotions get positive similarities and the negative emotions get negative similarities. {% endblock %}
